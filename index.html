Comprehensive Benefits of Membership

🚀 Free AWS Credits Gain access to free AWS credits for hands-on practice in real cloud environments at zero cost.
📜 Certification Support Receive structured preparation and guidance for AWS Cloud Practitioner and Solutions Architect certifications.
🛠 Hands-on Experience Work directly with core services like Amazon EC2, S3, Lambda, and RDS.
🌐 Industry Networking Connect with AWS engineers, professionals, and mentors during exclusive events.
💻 Live Projects Collaborate on project sprints and build real-time cloud-powered solutions for your professional portfolio.
🏆 Competition Access Get priority entry into AWS-hosted hackathons, buildathons, and innovation challenges.
👥 Mentorship & Leadership Access 1-on-1 mentorship and opportunities to hold leadership roles within the college community.
💼 Career Growth Strengthen your resume with recognized skills and gain access to internship opportunities in the cloud space.
